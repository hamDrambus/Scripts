1) Turn on 'stereo mixer' in sound recording devices

2) Turn off 'microphone' in sound recording devices

3) Launch Audacity

4) MME, Sound redirection.

5) Click at Recording level to start monotoring it

6) Adjust sound so that recorded track reach about 0.8 in amplitude.

7) Press record (red circle)

8) Start sound to be recorded.

9) Stop recording

10) Trim audio

11) Effects -> Normalize to 0.0 dB

12) File->Export Audio
